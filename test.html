Statistics Cheat Sheet

High School / Basic Statistics · Probability · Hypothesis Testing

Mean

x̄ = (1/n) Σxᵢ
Arithmetic average of all observations.
Example: (2 + 4 + 6) / 3 = 4

Variance

s² = (1/(n−1)) Σ(xᵢ − x̄)²
Measures the average squared deviation from the mean.

Standard Deviation

s = √s²
Indicates the typical spread around the mean.

z-Score

z = (x − x̄) / s
Standardized distance from the mean measured in SD units.

Probability

P(A) = |A| / |Ω|
Probability = favorable outcomes divided by all outcomes.

Conditional Probability

P(A|B) = P(A ∩ B) / P(B)
Probability of event A given event B occurred.

Expected Value

E(X) = Σ(xᵢ · pᵢ)
Long-run average outcome of a random variable.

Binomial Distribution

P(X=k)=C(n,k)pᵏ(1-p)ⁿ⁻ᵏ
Probability of exactly k successes in n trials.

Confidence Interval

CI = x̄ ± z · s/√n
Estimated range likely containing the true population mean.

Correlation

r = cov(x,y)/(sₓ·sᵧ)
Measures strength and direction of linear relationships.
Range: −1 to +1

Hypothesis Testing

p < α → Reject H₀
Small p-values indicate evidence against the null hypothesis.

68–95–99.7 Rule

μ ± 1σ = 68%
μ ± 2σ = 95%
μ ± 3σ = 99.7%
Quick rule for normal distributions.
